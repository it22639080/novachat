# Connect WhatsApp to NovaChat AI

Use this guide when a business owner wants to connect an official WhatsApp Business account to NovaChat AI.

## Steps

1. Log in to NovaChat AI.
2. Open Settings.
3. Go to WhatsApp Integration.
4. Select Connect Automatically.
5. Click Connect WhatsApp.
6. Log in with Facebook when the Meta window opens.
7. Choose your Meta Business.
8. Choose or create your WhatsApp Business Account.
9. Select and verify your WhatsApp phone number.
10. Allow the requested permissions.
11. Wait for NovaChat AI to show connection success.
12. Send a test message from the same page.
13. Configure your AI Assistant.
14. Add Knowledge Base documents if your AI should answer from business data.
15. Enable AI auto-reply when testing is complete.

## Notes

- NovaChat AI uses the official Meta WhatsApp Business Platform only.
- Your access token is stored securely and is not shown in the dashboard.
- If automatic connection is unavailable, support can use Manual Setup as an advanced fallback.
- Meta may require business verification, phone verification, template approval, or App Review before all features work in production.
